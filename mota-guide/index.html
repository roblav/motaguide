Home Page

In the first epic, we set out to replace the homepage map experience with a fresh design based on visuals supplied by the RAPP team. Given that the old map experience was powered by Adobe Flash and therefore not accessible to a wide range of users or search engines, one of the primary goals was to develop the map using modern accessible technologies.
